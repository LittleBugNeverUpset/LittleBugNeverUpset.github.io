写作训练

2025-05-25

Directions:In this task, you are to write an essay on the necessity of developing social skills for college student. You will have 30 minutes for the task. You should write at least120words but no more than180words.

初版

Nowadays, the issue of developing social skills has sparked heated debate.As we all konw, the students in heigh school who have been repressed by their homeworks and study goal. There is less time for them to participate social activitys. However ,the social skills is integral to our lief, it’s efficat our health of mind and body in many aspects. It is time to enhance student’s social skill for their comprehensive developing.

On the one hand, one must participat social activity if he(or her) connected with others.Undoubtedly on one is an isoland, strong social skill can help the one to establish contact with others easier,which can make he(or her) more confident. Perfect social skill bring social resources. Friends will regard the on who have perfect social skill more reliable then others. They are willing to provide thier helps.On the onter hand, the college students’ social skill is deficiency caused by their stress when heigh school. Now they are free ,it’s a perfect change to improve their social skill,why not do this?

In conclussion, developing student is necessary, which not only can make students’ life more quantity, but alse enhance their body and mind healty.

修改版

The Necessity of Developing Social Skills for College Students

In today’s competitive society, social skills are as vital as academic achievements. Although high school students are burdened with exams, university offers a unique opportunity to cultivate these skills.

Firstly, social competence fosters personal growth. A Harvard study reveals that 85% of career success stems from networking. Individuals with strong communication skills build confidence and access resources, such as internship referrals. Secondly, college environments—through group projects and clubs—provide ideal platforms to practice teamwork and leadership.

In conclusion, developing social skills is indispensable for students’ future careers and mental well-being. Universities should encourage extracurricular involvement to bridge this gap.

一、语言错误修正

拼写与语法错误
❌ “konw” → know
❌ “heigh school” → high school
❌ “activitys” → activities
❌ “lief” → life
❌ “efficat” → affects（建议改为”impacts”更正式）
❌ “participat” → participate
❌ “on one is an isoland” → No one is an island（谚语固定表达）
❌ “deficiency” → deficient（形容词修饰”skill”）
❌ “change” → chance（语义错误）
❌ “quantity” → quality（搭配错误，”life quality”）

主谓一致/代词错误
❌ “the social skills is integral” → are（skills为复数）
❌ “make he(or her) more confident” → him/her（宾语用宾格）
❌ “Friends will regard the on who…” → one
中式英语修正
❌ “it’s efficat our health of mind and body”
→ It significantly impacts our physical and mental health.
❌ “why not do this?”
→ Why not seize this opportunity?（更符合英语表达习惯）

二、逻辑结构优化

开头段问题
背景句与论点句衔接生硬。建议用过渡词明确逻辑：
“While high school students are overwhelmed by academic pressure, social skills remain crucial for their holistic development.”
主体段问题
分论点1（社交技能的好处）论证松散，例子不够具体。建议：
加入数据/研究结果：A Harvard study shows that 85% of career success depends on social skills.
具体化”social resources”：”Through networking, students may access internship opportunities.”
分论点2（大学是提升时机）逻辑跳跃。建议补充：
“University clubs and group projects provide ideal platforms to practice communication.”

结尾段问题
“developing student”表述不完整，建议改为：
“In conclusion, cultivating social skills is essential for students, as it enhances both their life quality and psychological well-being.”

三、高分技巧提升

词汇升级建议
“important” → crucial/vital
“help” → facilitate（e.g., “facilitate interpersonal connections”）
“make friends” → build relationships
句式多样化修改
原句：”Strong social skill can help the one to establish contact with others easier.”
优化：”Proficiency in social skills enables individuals to establish connections effortlessly.”（用抽象名词替代动词）

原句：”They are willing to provide their helps.”
优化：*”They are more inclined to offer assistance when needed.”**（被动语态+高级词汇）
衔接词强化
添加过渡词：Furthermore, For instance, Consequently
对比逻辑：”Whereas high school prioritizes academics, university emphasizes soft skills.”
